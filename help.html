The IFA Referee Evaluation form is a mobile-first web app for evaluating fistball referees during matches. Fill in match details, score the referee across 6 weighted criteria, and export the result via email to the IFA office.

The form works in 5 languages (English, German, Spanish, Portuguese, Italian) — toggle in the top-right corner of the header.

💡 The form remembers your scores if you navigate back from the result screen. All data is reset with 🔄 New Evaluation.

Fill in all fields before proceeding to scoring. This data appears in the final report and is stored in the IFA database.

💡 Complete all fields accurately — they appear in the final report and cannot be changed after export.

Each criterion starts at 10 points. Deduct 1 point per mistake. The minimum score is 1. Use the − and + buttons to adjust.

Scoring Scale

ScoreLabelMeaning
9–10ExcellentNo or negligible errors
7–8GoodMinor, isolated errors
5–6InconstantNoticeable, recurring errors
3–4PoorSignificant errors affecting the match
1–2CriticalSevere errors, unacceptable performance

The 6 Criteria

1. Rules×4

Practical application of the rules during the match. Highest weight — any significant rule mistake carries major impact.

2. Leadership×3

Penalties, disciplinary control, game control, communication with players & coaches, referee team management.

3. Technical Mgmt×1

Start & end of match (jerseys, game report, signatures), scoreboards, technical interruptions, unexpected situations.

4. Balls×1

Approval and management of ball exchanges during the match.

5. Demeanor×3

Hand signals, whistle volume and clarity, body language and presence on the court.

6. Motion & Behavior×2

Rally positioning, service positioning, net balls, cognitive aspect: observe → think → act.

⚠️ Rules (×4): A score below 8 automatically disqualifies the referee from IT1, regardless of the overall percentage.

Notes & Voice Input

Each criterion has an optional Notes field — tap the toggle to expand. Notes are included in the exported report. Always add notes when deducting points.

Voice input 🎤: Tap the microphone button to start dictation, tap again to stop. Transcribed text is appended to the notes field. Requires microphone permission (Chrome, Edge, Safari).

💡 "Missed fault call at 6:5 in set 2" is more useful than "missed calls" — be specific.

Score Calculation

Weighted sum = (Rules×4 + Leadership×3 + Technical×1 + Balls×1 + Demeanor×3 + Motion×2)
Weighted average = sum ÷ 14
Score% = ((average − 1) ÷ 9) × 100

Verdict Conditions

VerdictConditions
IT1 Score ≥ 90% · No criterion below 6 · At most 1 criterion exactly at 6 · Rules score ≥ 8
IT2 Score ≥ 85% · No criterion below 6 · At most 1 criterion exactly at 6
NQ Does not meet IT1 or IT2 requirements

Exporting

Before exporting, hold a brief feedback session with the referee — discuss observations, strengths, and deductions. Add any insights from that conversation to the Remarks field.

Tap ⬇ Export to open your email client with a pre-filled message to the IFA office. You must send the email — the evaluation is not submitted until the email is actually sent.

⚠️ Do not edit the email body — it contains machine-readable data. Any changes will break automated processing.
  • Evaluate in real time or immediately after the match — memory fades quickly.
  • Be specific in notes: "missed fault call at 6:5 in set 2" beats "missed calls".
  • Use the difficulty level honestly — a referee performing well in a Hard match should be rated accordingly.
  • Rules mistakes are weighted ×4 — a single critical error significantly impacts the final score.
  • A score of 6 on any criterion is a borderline pass — more than one such score blocks IT1 and IT2.
  • Voice input works best in a quiet environment — always review the transcription before saving.
  • After the match, hold a short feedback session with the referee before exporting and add observations to the Overall Remarks field.
  • Tap 🔄 New Evaluation on the result screen to reset all fields for the next referee.
